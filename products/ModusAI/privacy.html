Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 30, 2025

Modus AI is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles your data.

01. On-Device Pre-processing

The core of Modus AI’s spatial reasoning and image identification happens directly on your device. We leverage Gemini Nano and on-device machine learning to minimize the need for external data transmission. Your home photos are your business.

02. Data Collection

Modus AI does not collect personal identifiers like your name, address, or email unless you explicitly provide them for support or contractor brief exports. Photos uploaded to the "Big Bang" render engine are processed securely and are not used for model training by PaGuire.

03. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data to third parties. Data is only shared with third-party processing engines (like Google Gemini 1.5 Pro) for the sole purpose of generating your room renders.

04. Your Control

You have full control over the photos you upload and the renders you save. Renders are stored locally on your iOS device or in your personal iCloud if enabled.

05. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at Christopher.R.McGuire@gmail.com.
