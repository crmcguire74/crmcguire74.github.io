Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free AI interior design app?

Modus AI is free to download and works on a credit system. Unlike competitors that charge $20-$40/month, Modus AI uses a pay-as-you-go model, so you never pay for a subscription you aren't using.

Can AI design my room but keep my furniture?

Yes. Modus AI features a specific "Refresh & Elevate" mode that identifies your existing sofas, tables, and cabinets and redesigns the room around them, updating only the decor, paint, and lighting.

How can I visualize paint colors in my room?

Use the "Paint Only" mode in Modus AI. It uses texture mapping to apply new colors to your walls while preserving shadows and lighting conditions for a realistic preview.

Does this work for exterior home design?

Absolutely. Modus AI includes specialized models for Exterior Facades, Garden Landscapes, and Patios. It respects architectural boundaries to ensure generated ideas are structurally possible.
