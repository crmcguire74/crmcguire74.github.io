The Problem with Standard AI Design

Most AI design apps are just filters—they replace your favorite sofa with a random chair and suggest windows where load-bearing walls stand. They offer "vibes" but no real-world utility.

RoomCraft AI is different. Built by a Senior Engineering leader with a focus on spatial integrity, our app uses a proprietary Reasoning Pipeline to ensure every redesign is physically possible and personally relevant.

Why RoomCraft AI?

  • Intelligent Spatial Constraints respect existing room geometry.
  • Functional Organization Mode maximizes storage and layout optimization.
  • Precision Furniture Mapping via an advanced image-to-image pipeline.

Core Engineering Features

01. The "Furniture Lock" System

Identify and "lock" specific furniture pieces. Whether it’s a family heirloom or a brand-new sectional, our AI builds the new aesthetic around your existing assets.

02. Structural Integrity Toggles

Choose your level of transformation from Surface Level (paint/textures) to Structural Mode (wall removals/windows) with architectural accuracy.

03. Intelligent Organization Mode

AI-driven suggestions to maximize storage and optimize furniture placement for small-space living, focusing on flow and utility.

04. Precision Paint Visualizer

High-fidelity texture mapping shows exactly how light interacts with specific colors on your actual walls, maintaining shadows and depth.
