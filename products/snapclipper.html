SnapClipper: Capture. Organize. Remember.

SnapClipper removes the friction from knowledge capture. Everything you save is automatically categorized, searchable in seconds, and stored privately on your device. Whether you're studying, designing, coding, or researching, you get a calm dashboard that keeps ideas at your fingertips.

One-Click Saving: Highlight text anywhere, right-click, and save without popups or modal interruptions.

AI-Powered Organization: Automatic tagging across Research, Inspiration, Code, Quotes, News, Tutorials, Ideas, and Notes.

Beautiful Dashboard: A responsive workspace with dark mode, filters, and saved views for every device size.

Privacy First: Clips never leave your browser storage. We do not track activity or sell data—your knowledge stays yours.

Get Started in Seconds

A redesigned stepper makes onboarding clearer than ever.

  1. 1

    Install SnapClipper

    Add the Chrome extension, pin it to your toolbar, and grant clipping permissions.

  2. 2

    Highlight anything

    Select text on any website. SnapClipper preserves the surrounding context automatically.

  3. 3

    Right-click & clip

    Choose “Clip with Context,” or tap the keyboard shortcut to save without lifting your hands.

  4. 4

    Search instantly

    Open the dashboard, filter by tag or date, and keep moving with confidence.

Local storage reminder: export backups from Settings → Data & Storage for peace of mind.

Privacy & Security

Your clips live only inside this device’s browser storage. Export backups anytime from Settings → Data & Storage, and rest easy knowing there are no trackers, no ads, and no hidden sync processes.

Need screenshots with every clip? Enable Screenshot Capture and keep visual context without sending data to external servers.
