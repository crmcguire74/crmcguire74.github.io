SnapClipper: Capture. Organize. Remember.

SnapClipper removes the friction from knowledge capture. Everything you save is automatically categorized, searchable in seconds, and stored privately on your device. Whether you're studying, designing, coding, or researching, you get a calm dashboard that keeps ideas at your fingertips.

One-Click Saving: Highlight text anywhere, right-click, and save without popups or modal interruptions.

AI-Powered Organization: Automatic tagging across Research, Inspiration, Code, Quotes, News, Tutorials, Ideas, and Notes.

Beautiful Dashboard: A responsive workspace with dark mode, filters, and saved views for desktop, tablet, and mobile.

Privacy First: Clips never leave your browser storage. We do not track activity or sell data—your knowledge stays yours.

Privacy & Security

Your clips live only inside this device’s browser storage. Export backups anytime from Settings → Data & Storage, and rest easy knowing there are no trackers, no ads, and no hidden sync processes. SnapClipper is pure, private productivity.

Need screenshots with every clip? Enable Screenshot Capture and keep visual context without sending data to external servers.
